## ⚠️ RED FLAGS TO WATCH FOR

- **"We don't really have on-call"** — might mean they're not operationally mature
- **"We just need someone to execute tickets"** — conflicts with their stated "ownership" culture
- **Vague about architecture** — small teams should know their stack intimately
- **No mention of testing/deployment practices** — concerning for a platform company

---

## ✅ GREEN FLAGS

- Concrete examples of production incidents and learnings
- Engineers involved in architectural decisions, not just managers
- Discussion of observability, monitoring, alerting as first-class concerns
- Acknowledgment of technical debt and plan to address it
