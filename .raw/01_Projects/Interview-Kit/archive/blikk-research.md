## Recruiter Soft Questions

### 1. Swedish Language Requirement (CRITICAL)
> "The job description mentions Swedish fluency as a requirement. Given that I'm currently based in Gothenburg and working in English-only environments, how strict is this requirement? Is the team international or primarily Swedish-speaking?"

**What to listen for:**
- Is this a hard requirement or nice-to-have?
- Team composition (international vs. Swedish)
- Client-facing requirements driving the language need
- Possibility of English-only with Swedish learning support

### 2. Coding vs. Leadership Ratio
> "The role mentions not coding 100% of the time. Can you give me a realistic percentage? Am I looking at 30% coding/70% leadership, or more like 70% coding/30% leadership?"

**What to listen for:**
- Actual coding time allocation
- Whether this is an architecture role with occasional coding or hands-on dev with leadership overlay
- How "technical height" translates to day-to-day work

### 3. Frontend Stack Details
> "The job mentions fullstack experience and guiding both FE and BE architecture. What specific frontend technologies does the team use? React, Blazor, Vue, or something else?"

**What to listen for:**
- Current frontend stack
- Any planned migrations or changes
- Whether they're expecting deep frontend expertise or basic competence

### 4. Remote Work Reality
> "The posting mentions Gothenburg as a location but also says 'för rätt person kan det även finnas möjlighet att jobba helt remote.' How is this determined? What makes someone the 'right person' for remote vs. office-based?"

**What to listen for:**
- Remote policy clarity
- What criteria determine remote eligibility
- Office attendance expectations for remote workers
- Whether this is a negotiation point or firm policy

### 5. Team Structure & Size
> "Can you tell me about the team I'd be leading? How many developers? What's the seniority mix? Are there other Team Leads I would collaborate with?"

**What to listen for:**
- Team size (mentioned "två Team Lead Engineers" in posting - two positions)
- Senior/junior ratio
- Existing leadership structure
- Whether this is building a new team or leading existing one

### 6. "Servant Leader" Interpretation
> "The role emphasizes 'servant leader' approach. In practice, what does this look like at Blikk? How does decision-making work between Team Lead, Product Owner, and the team?"

**What to listen for:**
- Autonomy level for Team Lead
- Product vs. Engineering decision boundaries
- Whether this is truly servant leadership or traditional management with different name

### 7. Deadline Extension
> "I noticed the application deadline was March 5th. Has the position been filled, or is the search still open? What's the current timeline?"

**What to listen for:**
- Why they're reaching out after deadline
- How many candidates they're considering
- Interview timeline if moving forward
