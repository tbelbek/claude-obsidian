What Is a Loop Interview?    
The Loop Interview is a series of back-to-back conversations with members of our hiring team. You will meet with three interviewers, each focusing on two of our guiding principles. This helps us gain a well-rounded view of your experience and how you approach your work.  
  
What to Expect  
- Three one-on-one interviews with team members
- Approximately 45 minutes per interview
- Behavioral questions connected to the guiding principles most relevant to this role

  
How to Prepare    
- Bring real examples from your professional experience
- Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to structure your responses
- Be ready to discuss both your decision-making process and the outcomes you achieved
