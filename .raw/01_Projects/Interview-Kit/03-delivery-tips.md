# Delivery Tips

> [!tip] Interview sirasinda aklinda tut.

---

1. **"I" not "we"** — "the team did X, **I** did Y"
2. **Numbers** — 30%, 55%, 60%, 7x, 25 engineers, 3.1→3.7
3. **Don't volunteer detail** — let them ask, then open the relevant pillar
4. **Don't reuse stories** — RabbitMQ for failure, Volvo for pressure, etc.
5. **Match pillar** — architecture → [[10-pillar-software-dev]] | process → [[11-pillar-devops]] | people → [[12-pillar-leadership]] | ways of working → [[13-pillar-agile]]
6. **Honest on gaps** — "haven't used X, but understand the model, here's what's similar"
7. **Stories lookup** — [[ref-ways-of-working#Behavioral Stories — STAR Format]]
